Common questions

What are tokens?
Tokens are the unit of AI processing that powers every feature in ConstructWise. Think of them like fuel — every time the AI generates a bid, builds a schedule, analyzes a document, or answers a chat question, it consumes tokens. Simple tasks like a quick chat question use fewer tokens (~500–2K). Complex tasks like generating a full multi-trade bid estimate use more (~15K–30K). Your monthly allotment is designed to comfortably cover normal usage.
Why token-based pricing instead of a flat fee?
Every AI request costs us real compute — the more complex the task, the more it costs. Token-based pricing means you're never subsidizing another company's heavy usage, and we never have to throttle your access or cap your features. Your subscription includes a generous monthly allotment that covers most users. You only pay extra if you go significantly beyond that — and at that point, the value you're getting far exceeds the cost.
Will I run out of tokens?
Most users won't. On Foundation, 229,000 tokens covers roughly 8–15 full bid estimates plus daily chat usage and document analysis per month. On Podium, 650,000 tokens is built for high-volume shops running 25–40+ bids monthly. You'll get usage alerts at 80% and 100% so there are never any surprises.
Can I see my token usage in real time?
Yes. Every plan includes a usage dashboard that shows your current token consumption, a breakdown by feature (bids, schedules, chat, document analysis), and projected usage for the month. You'll also receive automated alerts at 80% and 100% of your allotment.
Do unused tokens roll over?
Tokens reset monthly and do not roll over. We intentionally sized each allotment with a comfortable buffer so you're not counting every query. If you consistently have leftover tokens, that means you're on the right plan.
Can I add more users later?
Yes. You can add users at any time and each user gets their own token allotment. On Foundation you can have up to 3 users, Podium supports up to 15, and Summit is unlimited. Read-only collaborators (e.g., project owners reviewing bids) are free and unlimited on every plan.
What integrations are available?
Foundation includes Excel and PDF export. Podium adds direct integrations with Procore, Primavera P6, Egnyte, QuickBooks, Sage, and more. Summit unlocks all 40+ connectors including HCSS, BIM 360, Viewpoint Vista, Smartsheet, and custom API access for your BI tools.
Is there a free trial?
Podium plan includes a 14-day free trial with full access. For Foundation, we offer a live demo where we'll build a real bid using your project data so you can see the value before committing. Summit customers get a custom pilot program.
